Our story

We’ve always been obsessed with making test automation better for people.

Software is changing the world every day. It's behind everything we do; powering back-office systems and websites, to robotics and mobile apps used across a wide range of devices. We want to help the people developing those powerful applications that are improving everyone’s lives.

We created Testim because automated testing is still difficult. Being developers for the last 20 years, we recognized we spend time and energy maintaining our automated testing environments, and are still anxious with how a simple bug fix might break another part of our application.

Testim is software that’s easy and works the way you want it to, supporting Agile transformations and shift left.

Our values

  • Be Your Own Customer

    Testim is built using Testim. We rely on the same platform and tools our customers pay us for. We strive to build products and design experiences we would want for ourselves.

  • Empower Individuals

    Testim believes in empowering our team members to create amazing things. Individuals create the foundation to support the innovations of the companies that chose to work with us. This innovation is what our product enables.

  • QA Is Not A Luxury

    Good software quality means fusing the many details into a package that is simple and user friendly. From the solutions we create to the experience our customers have, Testim strives for quality in test automation.

  • Good Work Takes Time

    We're preparing for the marathon. We respect the creative process and preparing our long term goals without fear. We only release our products when they meet our standards and not just each sprint.

  • Optimize Towards Ideals

    We're data driven. That data is critical for tracking and testing the performance of our business. The metrics are a reflection of our ideas and implementation in the market. What drives our decision making is our values and ideals.

  • Simplify

    We built Testim out of necessity to keep business simplified. To see our customers products delivered seamlessly with ease of use delivered with quality they can trust. We built quality test automation so you can focus on innovation.
