Full Stack Developer

We’re looking for a full stack developer with broad experience in large-scale distributed systems to help us build Testim.

Overview

You’ll be responsible for designing and developing our flagship product using cutting-edge technologies, implementing features that will change the way software companies automated their UI tests. You will be taking a significant part in a growing startup, where you will directly impact our product.

Testim is a highly technological product, so we’re looking for exceptional people to join us and bring the new generation of automation tool to the world!

Why you’ll love your job

  • You will be challenged to push your development skills to the limits by researching and using new technologies, APIs and frameworks.
  • You will work with cutting edge web technologies, in the highest levels.
  • Work with a proven technical team to deploy your code to production daily.
  • Be rewarded for your hard work with a competitive salary, stock options, benefits and all the coffee and beer you can drink.
  • Get in at the ground level to help influence the culture of the company.

Requirements and skills

3+ years of backend experience; programming languages: Javascript/Java/Python/C# and databases: MongoDB, MySQL, Redis.

3+ years of experience in developing web apps using a framework: Angular/React/Vue

Strong analytical and problem solving skills: solve complex problems, take a new perspective on existing solutions, exercise judgment based on the analysis of multiple sources of information

BSc in Computer Science or equivalent

Know how to take responsibility and a sense of ownership over the quality of the created product. A team player, self-motivated with an ability to work independently in a dynamic work environment.

Advantages

  • Good understanding of browser behaviour such as: DOM events, iframes, extension content scripts, etc
  • System architecture and design skills.
  • Experience with DevOps and Cloud infrastructure.
  • Experience with test automation frameworks such as Selenium.
